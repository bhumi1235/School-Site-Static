Rules & Regulations

Campus Discipline

General School Rules

To maintain a conducive learning environment, students are expected to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Punctuality: Students must arrive at least 10 minutes before the first bell.
  • Uniform: Neat and complete school uniform is mandatory on all working days.
  • Electronic Gadgets: Mobile phones and personal electronics are strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance is required for academic eligibility.

Disciplinary Protocol

Any breach of school etiquette will be dealt with according to the institutional disciplinary framework, involving parent-teacher consultations.

Financial Policy

Fee Regulations

Payment Schedule

All monthly fees must be cleared by the 10th of the respective month.

Late Fee Fine

A fine of ₹50 per day will be applicable for payments made after the due date.

Payment Modes

We accept Online Bank Transfer, UPI, and Cheque payments at the admin desk.

Safe Transit

Bus & Transport Rules

Safety is our priority. Students using the school transport must follow these safety protocols:

  • Always carry the valid Bus Pass for verification.
  • Stay seated and maintain silence during transit.
  • Board and deboard only at designated stops.
Ethics & Values

Code of Conduct

Evergreen International School stands for integrity, respect, and responsibility. We expect our students to be ambassadors of these values within and outside the campus.
