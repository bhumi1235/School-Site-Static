Rules & Regulations

Campus Discipline

General School Rules

To maintain a conducive learning environment, students are expected to adhere to the following guidelines:

Punctuality

Students must arrive at least 10 minutes before the first bell. Latecomers must report to the coordinator.

Uniform

Neat and complete school uniform is mandatory. Identity cards must be worn at all times on campus.

Leave Policy

Prior written application from parents is required for leave. Medical certificates are mandatory for leaves exceeding 3 days.

Property Care

Students must handle school property with care. Any damage will result in recovery of costs and disciplinary action.

Disciplinary Protocol

Any breach of school etiquette will be dealt with according to the institutional disciplinary framework. Serious offenses like bullying or vandalism may lead to immediate suspension or expulsion.

  • First Warning: Verbal counseling
  • Second Warning: Parent Consultation
  • Final Action: Disciplinary Committee Review
Financial Policy

Fee Regulations & Scholarships

Transparent fee structures and merit-based assistance ensure education remains accessible and structured for all our families.

Payment Deadlines

All monthly fees must be cleared by the 10th of the respective month. Payments after the 15th will incur a fine of per day.

Quarterly payments are eligible for a 5% discount if paid in advance.
Non-Payment Policy

Failure to clear dues for 3 consecutive months will lead to the removal of the student's name from rolls. Re-admission fees apply.

Participation in examinations is contingent upon 'No Dues' certification.
Refund & Scholarships

Admission and registration fees are non-refundable. Merit scholarships are available for students scoring in the top 5% of their grade.

Apply for financial aid by the start of the academic year.
Safe Transit

Bus & Transport Rules

Safety is our absolute priority. Global School provides GPS-enabled bus services with trained staff to ensure a secure commute.

Arrive at designated bus stops 5 minutes before scheduled time.
Authorized pick-up only to parents/guardians with valid ID cards.
In emergency, follow driver and conductor instructions strictly.

Misbehavior or damage to bus property will lead to immediate withdrawal of transport privileges.

Ethics & Values

Student Code of Conduct

Global School stands for integrity, respect, and responsibility. We expect our students to be ambassadors of these values within and outside the campus.

Respect & Inclusion

Zero tolerance for bullying, discrimination, or any form of harassment. Respect for diverse cultures and identities is mandatory.

Academic Integrity

Plagiarism, cheating, or any academic dishonesty will result in a zero grade for the assessment and parental notification.

Global Citizenship

Students are expected to contribute positively to the local and global community, upholding the name of Global School at all times.
