Academic Roadmap

A Holistic & Future-Ready Curriculum

At Global School, our curriculum is engineered to transcend traditional rote learning. We provide a rigorous academic foundation integrated with 21st-century competencies. Our framework is meticulously aligned with global standards, ensuring that every student is prepared for the challenges of higher education and professional life.

Core Sciences

Advanced Physics, Chemistry, and Biology modules with integrated laboratory research from early secondary levels.

Mathematics

Focus on logical reasoning, statistical analysis, and computational thinking across all grade levels.

Humanities

A deep dive into Global History, Literature, and Social Sciences to foster empathy and cultural awareness.

Specialized Track

Optional specialized modules in AI & Robotics, Data Science, and Environmental Sustainability.

The Global Advantage

Our "Dual Pathway" system allows senior students to choose between a research-heavy academic track or a skill-based vocational track, tailoring their education to their career aspirations.

  • Bilingual Proficiency Programs
  • Integrated STEAM Education

Pedagogical Excellence

Advanced Learning Methodologies

Project-Based Learning

We believe that concepts are best understood when applied. Our students engage in "Term Projects" where they solve real-world problems—ranging from urban planning models to renewable energy prototypes—integrating knowledge from multiple subjects.

Inquiry-Based Learning

Our classrooms are hubs of curiosity. Rather than simply delivering lectures, teachers pose stimulating questions that trigger student-led research. This method develops critical thinking, information literacy, and independent study habits.

Collaborative Synergy

Modern success is built on teamwork. Our "Synergy Sprints" encourage students to work in diverse groups, teaching them how to delegate, debate constructively, and achieve shared objectives while respecting varied perspectives.

A Multi-Dimensional Assessment Framework

Evaluation at Global School moves away from the anxiety of "High Stakes Testing." We utilize a Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) system that monitors a student's progress across academic and non-academic domains throughout the year.

Formative Assessments (FA)

Weekly quizzes, oral presentations, lab experiments, and portfolio reviews that allow for immediate feedback and correction.

Summative Assessments (SA)

End-of-term evaluations that measure depth of conceptual understanding and the ability to apply logic in complex scenarios.
